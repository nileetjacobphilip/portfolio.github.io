Let me introduce myself

Hi, My name is Nileet and I come from India. I have done my Bachelors in Computer Science Engineering and I have been working as a Software Product Engineer (UIUX Designer and Engineer) for the last couple of years. I started working as a front end developer and slowly transitioned into Design platform. During the advancement in my career I was fascinated by the importance of End-User Experience and I decided to pursue Masters in Human Computer Interaction.

After some research I came to know about the course, Masters in Interaction and Experience Design at University of Limerick. I was convinced in taking up the course to enhance my insights in designing skills and improve my employability, here I am, pursing the Masters course at UL and exploring the beautiful Limerick City. During my spare time I enjoy making Abstract Paintings, Canvas paintings, Glass paintings and explore different forms of art. I believe my interest lies within Art and Design field and other hobbies include photography and traveling.
